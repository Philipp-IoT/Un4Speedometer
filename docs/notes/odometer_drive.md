# Odometer Drive

The odometer is driven by a unipolar stepper motor. 

Motor Type: Epson MIM1A
Coil Resistance: 160 Ohms

| Pin | Terminal     | Connection      |
| --- | ------------ | --------------- |
| 1   | Light Grey 1 | Coil A - End 1  |
| 2   | Light Grey 2 | Coil B - End 1  |
| 3   | White        | Coil B - Center |
| 4   | Blue         | Coil A - End 2  |
| 5   | Red          | Coil B - End 2  |
| 6   | Orange       | Coil A Center   |
